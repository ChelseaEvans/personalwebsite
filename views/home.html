Chelsea Evans is a UX designer residing in Boston.

She loves consuming and interpreting the endless stream of new information that comes along with designing things for users in a society with ever-shifting needs and motivations.

She also enjoys bouts of physical and mental exploration, eating new foods in new places, drawing, being in bodies of water, daydreaming, plants and bugs and small things.
